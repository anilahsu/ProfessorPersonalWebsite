Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence is a method for the system to convey intelligence information through data processing and / or algorithm operations, thereby producing meaningful information and allowing machines to have smarter intelligence than humans. To put it simply, artificial intelligence is to teach computers to make "correct and smart decisions."

Machine Learning

Machine learning is one of the branches of artificial intelligence, allowing machines to learn automatically, find rules from huge amounts of data, and then have the ability to make predictions.

Data Exploration

Data exploration is the use of analysis techniques to discover unknown associations and rules between data, and artificial intelligence is one of the analysis techniques. You can use statistical methods for analysis, or you can use machine learning to process a large amount of data, and explore the correlation between multiple changing factors.
