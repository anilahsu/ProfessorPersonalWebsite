網路行銷與數據分析 E-Marketing and data analysis

網路行銷包含資訊技術與行銷觀念，這門課希望融合者兩面的觀點，在使同學了解網路行銷的理論與實務，輔以資料分析之技術，介紹目前常用的推薦系統、客戶分群等行銷資料分析，此外，也輔以期刊論文的研讀來加強同學的學術理論基礎。
E-Marketing involves information technology and marketing. The course will explore information technology/systems concepts from a non-technical perspective and examine how the marketing function is influenced by emerging technology trends. In addition, some famous e-marketing technique would be introduced in this course including recommender system, customer segmentation and so on. This course also discusses the journal paper to enhance the students' theoretical bases

商業智慧與管理 Business intelligent and management

商業智慧泛指應用資料倉儲設計與工具，協助企業從大量業務資料中尋找重要的資訊與知識，協助管理者建立決策。也會介紹數位金融最新趨勢，創新服務與最佳範例。
This course will focus on the business intelligent. Business intelligent is information systems that apply information technology to assist management in decision-making and strategic-planning. This course will introduce about the latest trends, innovations and best practices in digital finance.

系統分析與設計 System Design and analysis

介紹系統分析的基本概念及方法，深入熟悉開發一個電腦化資訊系統之方法及步驟。包含：系統分析、系統設計、系統建置、系統運轉、系統評鑑等之實施方式及步驟。
This course introduces the basic concepts and approaches of systems analysis including systems analysis, systems design, systems establishment, and systems evaluation and testing.
