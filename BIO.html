BIO-Sketch

Dr. Li-Chen Cheng is currently an Associate Professor of Department of Information and Finance Management, National Taipei University of Technology, Taipei, Taiwan (since 2019.8) She has disciplinary academic backgrounds and research experiences in AI, ERP and social science. Together with these experiences, her research interests focus on She is an advisor helping companies with digital transformation. Her research concentrates on include deep learning, opinion mining, financial technique, AI in E marketing, business intelligence and decision making models. The primary aim is to integrate AI technique in business problem.
Dr Cheng serves as an Associate Editor of Electronic Commerce Research and Applications (SSCI) and a reviewer for more than 10 academic journals. She has published papers in in well-recognized SSCI and SCI journals including Decision Sciences, Decision Support Systems, Electronic Commerce Research and Applications, Journal of Information Science, European Journal of Operational Research, Applied Soft Computing and many others.

At NTUT, she teach courses including System Design and analysis, Business intelligent and management, E-Marketing and data analysis.
She was previously a faculty member at Soochow University at Taiwan. She received her Ph.D. degree in information, management from National Central University.
She was also a short-term visitor scholar at George Washington University (GW) (2015) summer vacation.
